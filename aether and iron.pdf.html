Core Rules: Aether & Iron

1. Attributes

Players distribute points into four primary stats. Each stat influences specific "Save DCs."

Attribute Modifier Formula Use Case
Might $(Score - 10) / 2$ Melee, Athletics, Physical Saves
Agility $(Score - 10) / 2$ Stealth, Ranged, Reflex Saves
Essence $(Score - 10) / 2$ Magic Power, Mental Resistance
Wit $(Score - 10) / 2$ Investigation, Initiative

2. Combat Mechanics

Combat is turn-based. Action economy includes:

  1. Standard Action (Attack, Spell)
  2. Minor Action (Drink Potion, Draw Weapon)
  3. Reaction (Counter-spell, Parry)

Damage Calculation

The standard damage formula for physical strikes is:
$$Damage = (Base,Dice) + \text{Attribute Mod}$$
